Synopsis

Aarav Voss is an ordinary student whose dreams begin rewriting reality. As memories disappear and nightmares awaken, Aarav, Lyra and Kiro uncover an ancient force known as The Unwritten. Their journey leads into the Dream Realm where they must protect existence itself.
